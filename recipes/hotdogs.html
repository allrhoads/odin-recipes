Hot Dogs

Description

And who doesn't want to eat a grilled frankfurter in the summer months?! A great addition to any bbq party you've been invited to!

Ingredients

  • Your choice of frankfurters/hot dogs/sausages
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Relish
  • Grill (not for eating)
  • Charcoal (not for eating)
  • Hot dog buns

Steps

  1. Stack up your coals in the grill and set them ablaze.
  2. Open bag of franks and put them on a plate.
  3. Once the coals are glowing red, spread them out evenly in the grill.
  4. Put your franks on the grill.
  5. Grill until desired crispiness.
  6. Take off grill, put in bun and dress with whatever condiments you love.
