Sweet Restaurant Slaw

Description

This delicious sloppy slaw is the perfect treat to any barbeque. This tastes just like the cole slaw served at popular fried chicken or fish restaurants. It's excellent with burgers or on top of BBQ'd pork sandwiches, too!!!

Ingredients

  • slop
  • cabbage
  • Vinegar
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Little bit of beer

Steps

  1. Combine the coleslaw mix and onion in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk together the salad dressing, vegetable oil, sugar, vinegar, salt, and poppy seeds in a medium bowl; blend thoroughly. Pour dressing mixture over coleslaw mix and toss to coat. Chill at least 2 hours before serving.
  3. Eat up man!
