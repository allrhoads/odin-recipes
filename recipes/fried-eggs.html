Fried Eggs

Description

The perfect style egg for your next morning's breakfast! A fried egg is a perfect compliment for your buttered toast. It's easy to make, and supremely delicious!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Butter or oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the butter or oil in a skillet to medium heat.
  2. Add however many eggs you want to eat or however many will fit in skillet.
  3. If you want your eggs to be over easy, just let them cook until the sides get crispy.
  4. If you want your eggs to be over medium, very carefully flip the eggs. Take care not to break the yolk. Cook for one more minute.
  5. Carefully remove eggs from skillet and place on plate.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Eat up man!
